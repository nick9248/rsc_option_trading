Net Flow by Strike — Final Design

Horizontal bars, strikes on Y-axis. 2 traces only: Call Net & Put Net. Color = type, direction = sign.

Net Flow by Strike (Buy Vol − Sell Vol)  ·  BTC 27MAR26

Net = Buy Volume − Sell Volume  |  Positive → net buying  |  Negative → net selling

Call Net — one emerald bar per strike
Right of zero = calls net bought · Left = calls net sold
Put Net — one indigo bar per strike
Right of zero = puts net bought · Left = puts net sold
Legend: 2 items only — Call Net Flow (emerald) and Put Net Flow (indigo). Bar direction encodes sign — no need for separate buying/selling legend entries.
